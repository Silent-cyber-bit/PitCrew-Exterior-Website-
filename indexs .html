Our Services

From power washing to permanent lighting — Pitcrew handles everything on the outside so you don't have to.

💦
Pressure Washing

We blast away grime, algae, and stains from driveways, siding, patios, walkways, and more — leaving surfaces looking brand new.

🏠
Exterior House Cleaning

Full soft-wash and pressure cleaning of your home's exterior to remove dirt, mold, mildew, and oxidation from all surfaces.

Window Cleaning
